Hana Huie

  1. In Orbit
    May 10-12, 2019

    A duet investigating how energy transforms from microscopic to macroscopic through vibrations, relations, and space. Performed as part of the UMoves Undergraduate Dance Concert.
    Choreographers: Hana Huie & Dasha Mozolina
    Sound Designer: Dasha Mozolina
    Lighting Designer: Malory Hartman
    Costume Designer: Andrew Smith
    Performers: Hana Huie & Dasha Mozolina
    Venue: Dance Theatre at the Clarice

  2. Champagne Party for Time Travelers
    Jan. 31 - Feb 1, 2020

    A multimedia dance-theatre duet inspired by famed physicist Stephen Hawking's time travel experiment. What would happen if you tried to throw a champagne party for time travelers, and only release invitations after the party's conclusion? How would you feel sitting alone in that space, hoping for someone from the future to walk through the door?
    Choreographers: Hana Huie & Sydney Lemelin
    Sound Designer: Zoe Thomas
    Lighting Designer: Malory Hartman
    Costume Designer: Sydney Lemelin
    Performers: Hana Huie & Sydney Lemelin
    Venue: Dance Theatre at the Clarice

  3. Hell is a Bubble Tea Shop
    November 16-18, 2018

    Staged reading of a play exploring themes of Chinese-American identity, family, and mental health. Game show competitions, corrupted memories, and liminality intertwine in a bubble tea shop.
    Playwright: Hana Huie
    Director: Jasmine Mitchell
    Sound Designer: Anna Liddle
    Lighting Designer: Malory Hartman
    Costume Designer: Jennifer Kaznadi
    Performers: Kelli Barfield, Anthony Barnes, Kristen Ju, Elizabeth Prideaux, Zoe Thomas, Emily Zhou
    Venue: Cafritz Theatre at the Clarice
