Hell is a Bubble Tea Shop
"Hell is a Bubble Tea Shop". Staged reading of a play exploring Chinese-American identity, family, and mental health. Game show competitions, corrupted memories, and liminality intertwine in a bubble tea shop. Playwright: Hana Huie. Director: Jasmine Mitchell. Sound Designer: Anna Liddle. Lighting Designer: Malory Hartman. Costume Designer: Jennifer Kaznadi. Performers: Kelli Barfield, Anthony Barnes, Kristen Ju, Elizabeth Prideaux, Zoe Thomas, Emily Zhou. Venue: Cafritz Theatre at the Clarice.
"Breath, Boom" - an impressionistic play by Kia Corthron exploring Black womanhood, agency, and resistance in the face of all odds. Director: Jasmine Mitchell. Sound Designer: Dion Denisse Peñaflor. Lighting Designer: Ilana Simon. Costume Designer: Hana Huie. Performers: Kelli Barfield, Marcus Ford, Seairah Henry, Lillian Moki, Ibukun Oluwa-Ope, Kennedy Tolson. Venue: Cafritz Theatre at the Clarice.
